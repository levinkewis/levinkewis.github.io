About Me

Hey there!

Glad you can make it! My name is Kevin, I’m a graphic designer & illustrator who resides in Northwestern Ontario. I’m also a hobbyist photographer and 3D modeller. When it comes to design I like to break the rules; I’m experimental in that my color palettes don’t always compliment each other, they sometimes downright insult one another - but in the most visually pleasing way.
During my time in the Interactive Media Program (2018 - 2021), I’ve tried my hand at many other multimedia art forms such as web design, motion graphics, video and sound editing as well as visual effects.
Below you will find my IMD demo reel, my portfolio of design work from the past three years and my contact information with links to various social media platforms.

Good luck and go wild - LK
