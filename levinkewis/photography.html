Photography

A collection of shots for my photography class, edited in Adobe Camera Raw and retouched in Adobe Photoshop. The camera I used was a Nikon D5600 with a 35mm prime lens - for the beautifully blurred backgrounds. This was the final project for my photography class, the photo essay. The subject was to be chosen by us and naturally I'd chosen one of my favorite places in Thunder Bay to take photographs; The Centennial Conservatory.
