Graphic Design

Below you will find a collection of a few selected graphic design projects from my 1st and 2nd year of the IMD program. Some of the projects are simple illustrations created in Abobe Illustrator using the pen tool, while others are more complex - such as the Jeep Advertisement, which was created in Adobe Photoshop blending a multitude of images to fit a theme.
Other projects include a social justice themed storyboard and parallax backgrounds for Adobe Animate, a game menu created in Adobe Indesign and a carousel advertisement.
