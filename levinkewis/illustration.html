Illustration

A collection of illustrations for a client's Indigenous education website; For inspiration, I researched imagery and symbolism within First Nations cultures and how my findings relate to Northwestern Ontario - in an educational sense. First there were rough sketches, followed by preliminary drawings, concluding with final illustrations created in Adobe Photoshop with a Wacom drawing tablet.
